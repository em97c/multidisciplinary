a theoretical third project i've been modelling, this mid-century style home will be our family home going forward, once built.

our site is south-facing onto a main road, and so the project requires a careful balance of letting in light while maintaining privacy. to achieve this, i've opted for south-facing clerestory windows and a half-vaulted ceiling over the kitchen and living areas.

my interior model displays a black polished concrete floor and warm wooden ceiling/wall panelling. the kitchen features a serving window to the dining area and breakfast bar. planning permission for this project is pending. i am very excited to break ground on my third building project to-date.
